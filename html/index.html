How to Start

  1. Interact with the Rental NPC to rent a vehicle
  2. Once you have a vehicle, your delivery job starts automatically
  3. Follow the GPS to pickup boxes from your truck
  4. Deliver boxes to stores (3 boxes) or houses (1 box)
  5. Return to the NPC when finished to collect payment

Requirements

Valid driver's license
$500 rental deposit
Physical fitness for lifting boxes
